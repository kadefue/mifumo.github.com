Latest News & Research

Updates on our projects, deployments, and academic breakthroughs.

February 2026

Development of the SUA-MEIS Project

We are proud to be actively involved in the development of the Sokoine University of Agriculture Monitoring and Evaluation Information System (SUA-MEIS). This comprehensive software solution is designed to track organizational metrics and ensure stringent quality and performance standards are met across academic and administrative departments.

February 2026

Predictive Modeling for School Enrollment in Tanzania

Our data analytics team recently concluded work on a high-impact predictive modeling project aimed at forecasting school enrollment data across Tanzania. By applying advanced statistical modeling to demographic data, we are helping to provide actionable insights for national infrastructure and educational planning.

January 2026

Launch of "LindaPesa" AI Concept

We are thrilled to announce our latest AI initiative: LindaPesa. This concept utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to detect and prevent mobile money fraud in real-time. Designed specifically for the Tanzanian market, LindaPesa aims to secure digital financial transactions and protect consumers from emerging cyber threats.

January 2026

New Research Publication on the Mindu Reservoir

In our ongoing commitment to R&D and environmental sustainability, our team has co-authored a new research paper alongside Mr. Dickson Massawe. The research focuses on the Mindu Reservoir, applying modern data analysis techniques to study local ecological and water management challenges in Morogoro.
